The Story of Coffee

1600
🏛️
Coffee traveled from the Middle East to Europe, filling coffeehouses with lively discussions and cultural exchange.
1700
Colonial trade expanded plantations worldwide, transforming coffee from luxury into a daily ritual for many people.
☕
1900
⚙️
Instant coffee and espresso machines emerged, while global chains turned coffee into a universal lifestyle symbol.
2000
Third wave coffee focused on origins, sustainability, and craft, celebrating coffee as heritage and artisanal expression.
☕
a vintage journey that continues to shape our modern world
